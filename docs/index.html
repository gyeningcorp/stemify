How It Works

Three steps to understand any song

01
Upload Your Track
Drop in any MP3, WAV, FLAC, or M4A — up to 50MB. Works with songs you already have on your device.
02
AI Separates the Stems
Powered by Meta's Demucs — the same tech used in professional music production — your song splits into vocals, drums, bass, and other instruments.
03
Play & Read the Music
Solo any stem, mute the rest, and tap the notation button to see exactly what's being played in sheet music form — generated by Spotify's Basic Pitch.
